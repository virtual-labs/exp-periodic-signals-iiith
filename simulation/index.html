Fourier Series Coefficients

Instructions

To visualize how the number of fourier series coefficients considered can affect the signal:


Follow these steps:


  1. Choose a signal from the drop-down menu
  2. Enter any necessary parameter(s)
  3. Click the "Plot" button to view the plot in the figure


The "Parameter 1" and "Parameter 2" for the systems correspond to the following listed parameters:


Signal Parameter 1 Parameter 2
Sine Frequency Amplitude
Cosine Frequency Amplitude
Ramp - Amplitude
Pulse - Amplitude
Haar Scale Parameter Amplitude
Complex Exponential Frequency Amplitude
